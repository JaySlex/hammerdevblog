What is the game about?

The game is a sandbox moddable game. There is no specific goal, you can make or join a server. You can also play maps in solo.

Is there a possibility to get the game in advance?

At the moment, it is not possible to test or get a key for the game. The game is not playable enough for a public beta test.

How much will the game cost?

The game price will be around 10$ US, although this is not final and subject to change.

When will it be released?

I am a solo developer still in school, thus the game doesn't have a precise release date.
